## What is Oxara CMS?

Oxara CMS is a community management tool built to reduce the fragmentation that many communities face. Instead of managing your community across disconnected tools, Oxara brings the core aspects of numerous bots into one sleek dashboard.

You can use Oxara to manage members, organise staff, handle tickets, publish announcements, manage forms, track events, and review reports.

## How have we organised the documentation?

Documentation has been organised into a few simple sections:

- **Getting Started** helps you create your community and find your way around our dashboard.
- **Core Concepts** explains the important ideas behind Oxara, including communities, members, roles, permissions, notifications, and audit logs.
- **Features** covers our tools within the CMS.
- **Configuration** explains how to customise and secure your community.
- **Integrations** shows how Oxara integrates with external platforms.
- **Support** helps you troubleshoot and find help when needed.
- **Legal** covers the important stuff nobody wants to read. Sorry.
