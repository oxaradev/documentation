## What you will set up

When getting started with Oxara CMS, you will usually:

- Create or access your tenancy
- Review your dashboard
- Invite staff members
- Configure roles and permissions
- Review your core settings
- Connect any required integrations

<Note>
Do not want to configure everything right away? Start with the basics. Add more features as your community needs them.
</Note>

## Before you begin

Before setting up Oxara CMS, it helps to have:

| Item | Why it helps |
| --- | --- |
| **Community name** | Used when creating your tenancy. |
| **Brand details** | Helps make Oxara feel like yours. |
| **Staff list** | Helps you invite the right people early. |
| **Permission structure** | Helps you decide who should access each area. |
